View Quality Score

Estimates visual amenity potential using ocean proximity, elevation, greenery, and building density from open satellite data.

Try It

What It Measures

The View Quality Score estimates how good the visual outlook is from a location. It combines five factors from open satellite datasets, each measuring a different aspect of visual amenity.

85+Exceptional
70-84Great
55-69Good
40-54Average
25-39Limited
0-24Obstructed

Five Factors

FactorWeightData SourceWhat It Measures
Ocean Proximity3.0Overture Maps WaterDistance to nearest ocean, bay, or strait

Elevation Advantage2.5Open-Meteo DEMHeight above surrounding terrain
Green Space2.0Overture Maps POIsParks, gardens, reserves within 1km
Building Openness2.0Overture Maps BuildingsInverse of building density within 300m

Adaptive Weighting

Factors without available data are excluded from the weighted average rather than penalized. Inland locations are scored on 4 factors instead of 5.
